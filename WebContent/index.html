VirtualKinetik TM

VirtualKinetik started as a wearable technology Hackathon project composed of Team diVRse members. We are now working on developing multiple applications based on our original demonstrated concept: Rendering a 3-D object or environment in Virtual Reality and manipulating the object using hand gestures!

HARDWARE
The Rift uses custom tracking technology to provide ultra-low latency 360¡ head tracking, allowing you to seamlessly look around the virtual world just as you would in real life. Every subtle movement of your head is tracked in real time creating a natural and intuitive experience.
We are the Myo gesture control armband to read muscle activity so you can control software with gestures and motion.

Virtual resuscitation

Most people who experience cardiac arrest at home, work or in a public location die because they don't receive immediate CPR from someone on the scene. This is mostly due to lack of training and confidence from surrounding bystanders. Our system uses wearable technology to effectively teach CPR and save lives.

Conceptual video produced by Maya to demonstrate sound effect and animation of heart beats.
PS: Soundtrack "Staying alive" is used for professional CPR training

Products seek to reproduce a realistic experience using step-by-step first person POV with instructions describing approved CPR procedures.

Sound effects and timer guides players through an immersive and slightly stressful experience.

Virtual, 3-dimensional CPR training environment and tracking responder's actions using motion control with Myo.
